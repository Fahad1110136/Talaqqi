About

Talaqqi is an online platform designed to make Qur’an learning accessible and interactive. It brings the traditional teacher-student method into the digital world with live instruction and AI-powered recitation feedback.

Students can join real-time classes, explore a synchronized digital mushaf, view annotations, and track progress. Talaqqi blends authenticity with modern tools to deliver a reliable and enriching learning experience.

Features Overview

  • Secure Accounts: Easy signup with verified teachers.
  • Live Classes: Real-time audio/video teaching sessions.
  • Digital Mushaf: Dynamic highlighting for guided recitation.
  • Annotations: Teachers can add notes and explanations.
  • AI Recitation Feedback: Instant Tajweed and accuracy analysis.
  • Progress Tracking: Personalized recommendations and insights.
  • Role Management: Separate roles for students, teachers & admins.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide authentic Qur’an education to learners everywhere. Talaqqi supports students through guided recitation, structured learning, and intelligent technology—helping them improve and stay connected with qualified teachers globally.
